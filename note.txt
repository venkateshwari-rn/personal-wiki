========================================
  HOW TO RUN THE PERSONAL WIKI - DAILY
========================================

--- STEP 1: START THE APP (Every day when you want to use it) ---

Open Terminal and run:
  cd "/Users/venkateshwarinavaneetham/Desktop/wiki/Personal Wiki with LLM"
  pnpm dev

Then open your browser and go to: http://localhost:5173


--- STEP 2: DAILY EMAIL DIGEST (Runs automatically - no action needed) ---

The system is set up to automatically send emails every day at 7:00 AM SGT
via GitHub Actions. It does NOT require your laptop to be on.

What gets sent automatically:
  - Singapore CNA news digest (top 10 articles summarized by AI)
  - Your personal wiki digest (creative summary of your wiki pages)
  - Delivered to: rnvenkateshwari@gmail.com

GitHub Actions workflow file:
  ~/personal-wiki/.github/workflows/daily-digest.yml


--- STEP 3: SEND EMAIL MANUALLY (optional - for testing) ---

1. Start the app (Step 1 above)
2. Go to http://localhost:5173
3. Click the "Maintenance" tab
4. Click "Send News Digest Now"  → sends CNA Singapore news email
5. Click "Send Test Email Now"   → sends wiki digest email
6. Check your inbox at rnvenkateshwari@gmail.com


--- STEP 4: STOP THE APP (when done) ---

In the terminal where pnpm dev is running, press:
  Ctrl + C
